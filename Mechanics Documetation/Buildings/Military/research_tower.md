# Research Tower

## Overview
The Research Tower is a military building that grants players access to permanent technology upgrades providing wide-ranging benefits. Once built, it cannot be demolished. The Research Tower offers various research branches including military, economy, and specialized improvements that enhance overall gameplay effectiveness.

## Building Levels (Classic Era)

### Level Requirements and Stats

| Level | Player Level Required | Cost | Research Speed | Key Unlocks |
|-------|---------------------|------|----------------|-------------|

## Research System Mechanics

### Core Concepts
- **Permanent Upgrades**: All researched technologies are permanent
- **Resource Cost**: Technologies require Wood, Stone, Coins, or combinations
- **Research Time**: Each technology has a specific research duration
- **Prerequisites**: Many technologies require prior research completion
- **Level Requirements**: Building level determines accessible technologies

### Research Speed Modifiers
- Base research speed increases with building level
- Additional bonuses available through:
  - Decorations with research speed bonuses
  - Special events
  - Build items with research effects

## Research Branches (Pre-2018)

### Military Research Branch

#### Troop Training & Combat
**Elite Training** (Level 2+)
- Improves soldier training efficiency
- Reduces recruitment time
- Multiple upgrade levels

**Marching Formation** (Level 2+)
- Increases army movement speed
- Reduces travel time for operations
- Critical for quick response and raiding

**Maneuver** (Upgradeable to Level 7)
- Enhanced tactical positioning
- Improved combat effectiveness
- Multiple tiers for progressive improvement

**Chivalry** (Level 1+)
- Bonus to melee soldier combat strength
- Percentage-based improvement
- Stacks with other bonuses

**Medicine**
- Reduces soldier casualties in combat
- Improves army sustainability
- Valuable for sustained campaigns

**Fire Pump**
- Defensive improvements
- Reduces damage from certain attack types

#### Veteran Units (Level 2+)
**Veteran Swordsman**
- Unlocks elite version of basic swordsman
- Enhanced combat statistics
- Higher training cost but superior performance

**Militia**
- Special defensive unit
- Garrison-focused capabilities
- Cost-effective defense option

#### Specialized Military
**Ambulance Coach**
- Reduces soldier recovery time
- Improves casualty survival rates
- Supports sustained military operations

**Conscription**
- Increases soldier recruitment capacity
- Faster army building
- Essential for large-scale warfare

### Economy Research Branch

#### Resource Production
**Wheelbarrow** (Level 1+)
- Increases resource production efficiency
- Affects all resource types
- Fundamental economic upgrade

**Workbench** (Upgradeable to Level 9, later expanded)
- Significantly increases tool production speed
- Affects both Defense and Siege Workshops
- One of most important research technologies
- Multiple upgrade levels for progressive improvement

**Reinforced Axle** (Upgradeable to Level 10)
- Improves cart capacity
- Increases resource gathering efficiency
- Stacks with other bonuses

**Steel Tires** (Upgradeable to Level 10)
- Further cart improvements
- Enhanced resource transportation
- Complements Reinforced Axle

**Tree Nursery**
- Increases wood production
- Percentage-based improvement
- Stacks with building bonuses

**Plow**
- Increases food production
- Essential for large armies
- Supports sustained military operations

**Pickaxe**
- Increases stone production
- Complements quarry buildings
- Important for construction projects

#### Resource Management
**Shady Business** (Upgradeable to Level 7, later Level 10)
- Reduces resource costs
- Affects building and recruitment
- Valuable for rapid expansion

**Forced March** (Upgradeable to Level 5, later Level 10)
- Increases army speed
- Reduces travel time
- Improves raid efficiency and response time

**Scout**
- Enhanced reconnaissance capabilities
- Better intelligence gathering
- Complements Tavern operations

**Ranger**
- Advanced scouting benefits
- Extended vision range
- Improved map awareness

**Treasure Seeker**
- Increases loot from attacks
- Improves raid profitability
- Essential for resource farming

#### Special Economy
**Loot Log**
- Bonus resources from NPC attacks
- Increases farming efficiency
- Valuable for resource generation
- Note: Later restricted to NPCs only (2017)

**Genius** (Upgradeable to Level 5, later Level 10)
- Increases experience point gains
- Faster level progression
- Accelerates overall advancement

**Artisan Blacksmith**
- Improves equipment quality
- Enhanced combat bonuses
- Supports commander effectiveness

**Minstrel** (Upgradeable to Level 5, later Level 10)
- Increases glory point gains
- Accelerates glory progression
- Important for competitive rankings

**Biographer** (Upgradeable to Level 5, later Level 10)
- Additional experience bonuses
- Stacks with Genius
- Maximizes leveling speed

## Research Strategy

### Early Game Priorities (Levels 1-30)
1. **Wheelbarrow**: Foundation of economy
2. **Chivalry**: Basic combat improvement
3. **Workbench Level 1-2**: Tool production essential
4. **Elite Training**: Faster army building
5. **Marching Formation**: Improved mobility

### Mid Game Priorities (Levels 30-60)
1. **Workbench Higher Levels**: Maximize tool production
2. **Veteran Swordsman**: Enhanced military capability
3. **Maneuver**: Combat effectiveness
4. **Shady Business**: Cost reduction becomes more valuable
5. **Steel Tires/Reinforced Axle**: Maximize resource efficiency
6. **Treasure Seeker**: Improve raid returns

### Late Game Priorities (Level 60+)
1. **Max Workbench**: Critical for tool production
2. **All Combat Upgrades**: Maximize military power
3. **Advanced Economy**: Complete economic research
4. **Specialized Technologies**: Fill out research tree
5. **Veteran Units**: Elite army composition

## Strategic Considerations

### Research Order Optimization
- **Foundation First**: Core economic and military techs
- **Synergy Focus**: Research complementary technologies together
- **Cost-Benefit Analysis**: High-impact research first
- **Long-Term Planning**: Queue research for continuous progression

### Resource Management
- **Plan Ahead**: Research costs can be substantial
- **Stockpile Resources**: Ensure sufficient resources before starting
- **Balance Investment**: Don't neglect military for economy or vice versa
- **Event Timing**: Consider research during resource events

### Technology Synergies
- **Economic Synergies**: Wheelbarrow + Resource-specific research
- **Military Synergies**: Elite Training + Chivalry + Maneuver
- **Tool Production**: Workbench + Forge + Public Order
- **Raiding Efficiency**: Forced March + Treasure Seeker + Reinforced Axle

## Important Research Technologies

### Workbench (★★★★★ Critical)
- **Impact**: Dramatically increases tool production speed
- **Priority**: Highest priority technology
- **Levels**: Multiple levels, each providing significant boost
- **Application**: Affects both defensive and offensive tool production
- **Long-term**: Essential for competitive play

### Treasure Seeker (★★★★★ Critical)
- **Impact**: Increases resources looted from attacks
- **Priority**: Essential for resource-focused players
- **Application**: Makes raiding significantly more profitable
- **Synergy**: Works with Forced March for efficient farming

### Chivalry (★★★★ Very Important)
- **Impact**: Direct combat strength increase
- **Priority**: Early military improvement
- **Application**: Benefits all melee soldier attacks
- **Scaling**: Percentage-based, grows with army size

### Marching Formation (★★★★ Very Important)
- **Impact**: Reduces travel time for all operations
- **Priority**: High value for active players
- **Application**: Faster attacks, quicker responses
- **Synergy**: Works with Forced March for maximum mobility

### Maneuver (★★★ Important)
- **Impact**: Tactical combat improvements
- **Priority**: Mid-game military enhancement
- **Levels**: Multiple upgrades available
- **Application**: General combat effectiveness

## Research Completion Times

### Time Management
- Research times vary significantly by technology
- Plan research queue for continuous progress
- Can speed up completion with rubies (not recommended for small research)
- Balance short and long research for steady advancement

### Optimal Queuing
- **Short Research**: Fill gaps between major research
- **Long Research**: Start before periods of inactivity
- **Progression**: Queue complementary technologies
- **Resource Timing**: Align with resource availability

## Historical Development (Pre-2018)

### Initial Release (February 2012)
- Basic research tree with fundamental technologies
- Two-branch system (Military and Economy)
- Foundation of permanent progression system

### Early Expansions (2012-2013)
- March 2012: Added Wheelbarrow and Chivalry
- October 2012: Level 2 Research Tower, new technologies
- July 2013: Added Veteran units and specialized research
- December 2012: Bug fixes for veteran unit information

### Mid-Era Development (2014-2015)
- August 2014: Level 3 Research Tower, advanced veterans
- March 2015: Level 4 Research Tower, economic technologies
- Multiple level cap increases for key technologies
- Expanded research tree depth

### Refinement Period (2016-2017)
- June 2016: Technology rearrangement for better flow
- August 2016: Build item related technologies
- September 2016: Further reorganization
- February 2017: Swapped branch positions (Economic/Military)
- July 2017: Loot Log restricted to NPCs
- August-October 2017: Level cap increases for key technologies
- September 2017: Build item integration

## Tips and Best Practices

### Research Management
1. Always have active research
2. Plan research path in advance
3. Stockpile resources for critical research
4. Don't use rubies on short research
5. Research during downtimes (overnight, work hours)

### Resource Efficiency
1. Complete economic research early
2. Max Workbench before major military campaigns
3. Balance military/economic investment
4. Consider research costs in overall budget

### Competitive Advantage
1. Prioritize high-impact technologies
2. Complete military research before major conflicts
3. Economic research supports sustained campaigns
4. Specialized research for specific strategies

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
1. Neglecting Workbench research
2. Delaying economic technologies
3. Rushing military without economic foundation
4. Not planning research progression
5. Wasting rubies on minor research
