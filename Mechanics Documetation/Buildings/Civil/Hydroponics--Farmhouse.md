# Farmhouse - Good Game Empire

## Overview

The **Farmhouse** is a civil building that produces food, which is essential for maintaining soldiers (they desert without adequate food supply). It's a mandatory building for game progression and has **10 levels** total.

## Key Game Mechanics

### 1. Building Categories
- **Civil Building**
- **Smashable Building** (can be destroyed in attacks)

### 2. Placement Options
- Can be placed in the **Relic Food District** (as of September 2024)
- Regular castle placement

### 3. Late-Game Strategy
At legendary levels, it's recommended to demolish Farmhouses and replace with:
- **Granary**
- **Relic Greenhouse**
- **Relic Conservatory**

*These provide superior food production at higher levels.*

## Strategic Insights

### Early Game
- Essential for basic food production
- Take advantage of reduced construction times

### Mid Game
- Scale up all farmhouses to maximize food output
- Balance wood and stone resource allocation

### Late Game
- Replace with more efficient buildings (Granary, Relic buildings)
- Consider placement in Relic Food District for bonuses

### Key Considerations
- The building becomes **less efficient** at higher levels due to escalating costs vs. linear production increases
- Low-level players benefit from **dramatically reduced construction times** to accelerate early progression
- Food production is **critical** - soldiers will desert without adequate supply
